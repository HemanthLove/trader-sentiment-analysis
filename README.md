# 📊 Trader Performance vs Market Sentiment Analysis

## 📌 Objective

This project analyzes how Bitcoin market sentiment (Fear/Greed Index) impacts trader behavior and performance. The goal is to uncover patterns that can help improve trading strategies.

## ⚙️ Methodology

* Cleaned and preprocessed both datasets
* Converted timestamps into daily format
* Merged trading data with sentiment data based on date
* Created key metrics:

  * Daily Profit & Loss (PnL)
  * Win rate
  * Trade frequency
  * Average trade size
  * Long/Short ratio
* Performed analysis to compare trader performance across different market sentiments

---

## 📈 Key Insights

* Trader performance varies significantly between Fear and Greed market conditions
* During Greed periods, traders tend to trade more frequently and take higher risks
* Fear periods show more conservative behavior with reduced trade sizes and lower activity

---

## 💡 Strategy Recommendations

* Reduce leverage and risk exposure during Fear periods to avoid losses
* Avoid overtrading during Greed periods despite higher activity
* Focus on consistent traders rather than high-frequency traders for stable returns
